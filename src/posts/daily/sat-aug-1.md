### Notes

(See below for all authors.)	

> What offices doth Christ execute as our Redeemer? Christ, as our Redeemer, executeth the offices of a prophet, of a priest, and of a king, both in his estate of humiliation and exaltation. 

\- Westminster Shorter Catechism #23

> It ought to be observed, that the Son of God voluntarily entered the temptations...and fought, as it were, in a single combat with the devil, that, by his victory, he might obtain a triumph for us. Whenever we are called to encounter Satan, let us remember, that his attacks can, in no other way, be sustained and repelled, than by holding out this shield: for the Son of God undoubtedly allowed himself to be tempted, that he may be constantly before our minds, when Satan excites within us any contest of temptations. 

\- John Calvin, Commentary on Matthew, Mark, and Luke
